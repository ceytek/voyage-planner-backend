Common Issues

Trip Generation Not Working

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure you have sufficient credits
  • Try selecting different dates or destinations

Photo Recognition Issues

  • Make sure the photo is clear and well-lit
  • Ensure the landmark is visible in the image
  • Try taking a new photo from a different angle

Credits & Purchases

  • Credits are purchased through Apple In-App Purchase
  • For refund requests, contact Apple Support
  • Credits remain in your account indefinitely

Account Issues
