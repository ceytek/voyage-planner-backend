# Privacy Policy for Voyage - AI Travel Planner

**Last Updated:** November 17, 2025

## Introduction

Voyage ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: For account creation and communication
- **Name**: To personalize your experience
- **Profile Picture**: Optional, for account customization

### Usage Data
- **Trip Preferences**: Destinations, dates, interests you select
- **Credit Balance**: In-app purchase records
- **Saved Trips**: Your created itineraries and favorites
- **Photos**: Images you upload for landmark recognition (processed temporarily, not stored)

### Location Data
- **City/Country Selection**: Based on your trip planning choices
- We do NOT track your real-time location

### Authentication Data
- Firebase Authentication tokens
- Apple Sign-In credentials (if using Apple login)
- Google Sign-In credentials (if using Google login)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Generate personalized travel itineraries
- Provide landmark recognition via photos
- Manage your credit balance and purchases
- Send important updates about your account
- Improve our AI recommendations
- Ensure app security and prevent fraud

## Data Storage & Security

- **Backend**: Hosted on secure servers with encryption
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with industry-standard security
- **Authentication**: Firebase Authentication (Google/Apple certified)
- **Payment Processing**: Apple In-App Purchases (we don't store payment details)

## Third-Party Services

We use the following services:
- **Firebase**: Authentication and analytics
- **OpenAI**: AI-powered trip generation (no personal data shared)
- **Apple In-App Purchases**: Payment processing
- **Unsplash**: Stock images for destinations

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request data deletion
- Export your trip data
- Opt-out of marketing emails
- Delete your account at any time

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Photos**: Processed in real-time, not permanently stored

## Children's Privacy

Voyage is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page.
